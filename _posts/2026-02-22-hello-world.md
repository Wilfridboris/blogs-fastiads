Welcome to the **fastiads blog** — practical guides and insights for people who run paid advertising.

No filler. No generic advice. Just the specific information you need to set up, run, and improve ad campaigns on Meta, Google, TikTok, and beyond.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Platform setup guides** — step-by-step walkthroughs so you don't get tripped up by the prerequisites and gotchas most guides skip
- **Automation and optimization** — how to use rules, AI tools, and automation to manage campaigns without being glued to a dashboard
- **Campaign strategy** — targeting, creative, bidding, and everything that determines whether your campaigns actually work
- **Platform updates** — when Meta, Google, or TikTok changes something that affects how you run ads, we'll cover what it means practically
